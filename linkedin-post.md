🚀 Thrilled to introduce **CivicShield**, a robust platform tackling the India election literacy problem head-on! Ensuring voters have accurate, verifiable information about the Electoral Commission of India (ECI) process is essential for our democracy.

Built with a "Security-First" state-of-mind, CivicShield goes beyond traditional chat apps:
✅ **Fact-Checking verified with Gemini Native Grounding**: No more hallucinations. Claims about voting procedures, EVMs, or NOTA are verified using Google Search grounding, returning definitive verdicts backed by concrete sources.
✅ **Edge-Ready Security**: Implemented with Upstash Redis rate-limiting (20 req/min) to prevent abuse and handle high traffic seamlessly.
✅ **Enterprise Scalability**: Deployed on Google Cloud Run (asia-south1) for incredible latency. Integrated GCP Cloud Logging strictly outputs structured JSON, meaning our operations team can query anomalies at scale with zero friction.
✅ **Accessible to All**: WCAG 2.1 AA compliant UI, keyboard-navigable timeline steppers, screen-reader optimized, and supporting multiple languages (English, Hindi, Marathi).

By combining Next.js 14 App Router, Gemini 1.5 Pro, and Tailwind, we're not just building a tool; we're empowering citizens securely and reliably.
